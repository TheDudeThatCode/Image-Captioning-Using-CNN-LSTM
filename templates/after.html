Capt_The_Pic

Capt The Pic is a image captioning web app which will convert the uploaded image into a sentence. To build the model I have used ResNet50 and LSTM. I have used ResNet50 to extract the image features. LSTM is mainly used as language model to predict the next word. I have used feature vector of image and current word for predicting the next word
